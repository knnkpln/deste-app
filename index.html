Deste

Batak · Pişti · Onbirli · King — offline & ad-supported

Deste brings the most popular Turkish card games together in a single, offline-first mobile app. Play against intelligent AI opponents anytime, anywhere — no internet required.

Games included

  • Batak — bidding, paired, buried, and spade-trump variants
  • Pişti — the classic fast-paced card game
  • Onbirli — strategic favorite for tactic lovers
  • King — the elegant trick-avoidance game

Key features

  • Fully offline — no internet connection required
  • Intelligent AI bots that play like real opponents
  • Smooth animations and realistic card physics
  • Clean, focused interface
